Workouts

DAY ONE DISTANCE Aerobic Strength 6 X 800's Step downs (Start @ above race pace increase pace by10-sec per 800 until the last continuous interval is running the fastest). Examples: 5:30 -5:45 Range- 3:50-3:40-3:30-3:20-3:10-3:00 5:25 -5:00 Range - 3:40-3:30-3:20-3:10-3:00-2:50 4:59 -4:28 Range - 3:30-3:20-3:10- 3:00-2:50-2:40 COOL DOWN MID-DIST (400-800) 4X600 (Come through 400@ 1:00/60 sec) R 4-5min repeat! 10-12 min recovery then 4 X 200 @ 30-32 sec 3 min rec. Cool Down SPRINTER/HURDL/JUMPER 2 sets of 2 X 200 ; 3 X 150 Circuit of Cals (100 Pushups -5sets; Scorpions 40; 2x 1min planks front & laterals. Cool Down! DAY TWO DISTANCE EASY 3-4 MILES DAY TWO 400-800 2 sets of 3 X Broken 400 (Run 200 m jog 100m walk 100m repeat) rest 5 -8min btw sets! Cool Down DAY TWO JUMPERS/SPRINTERS/HURDLERS 2 sets of 3 x 20 m; 3 sets of 30 m; 3 x 40 meters (3pt or 4pt stance) Plyos - 2 sets of alternate leg bounds over 30m; 2 sets of 15 burpees! Cool Down DAY THREE DISTANCE FARTLEK - with 3min running on @ threshold pace w 2min off (recovery) This should be continuous for 30-40 min! CoolDown (400-800) 2 sets of 3 X 400's (Endless style- tag if there is no baton) Cool Down SPRINTERS/JUMPERS/HURDLERS Walk Turns Sprint Straights 75% effort 3 sets X 2 Laps Cool Down If you can not get to a Track go to a field that is flat! Be careful! Take Care! Coach
